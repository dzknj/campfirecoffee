General Location Info. and Plans for Expansion

"Campfire Coffee" is a 6 year old company. It has 5 locations located in Seattle metro as well as the greater Seattle Area. Plans are in the works to open more shops in areas such as North Seattle, Lake City Way, Edmonds, Mountlake Terrace and also Bellvue. Talks have even been underway about a possible opening on the East Coast in New York City, as well as Northern New Jersey. "Campfire Coffee" also has an online store where customers can order Bags of Coffee as well as general merchandise such as t-shirts, hats and mugs.
